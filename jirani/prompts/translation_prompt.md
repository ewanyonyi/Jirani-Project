# Translation Prompt

Use this prompt for translation and localization in the Jirani app.

## Purpose
Help the app translate conflict descriptions, mediation suggestions, and agreement summaries between English and local languages such as Swahili, while maintaining cultural sensitivity and respectful tone.

## Instructions
- Preserve the original meaning and tone
- Use simple, clear wording suitable for low-literacy contexts
- Keep the translation culturally appropriate and non-judgmental
- Prefer community-friendly phrasing over technical legal terms
- Avoid exposing any personal identifiers or private details

## Output guidance
- Specify source and target language if needed
- Keep translated text concise and easy to understand
- Use local phrases that support calm communication and cooperation
- When translating safety-related content, preserve warning tone without panic

## Example
Input: “Our field access is blocked, and people are worried about water.”
Output: Swahili translation that keeps the meaning clear and respectful.
