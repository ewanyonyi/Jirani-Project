# Agreement Summary Prompt

Use this prompt to generate clear, neutral summaries of community agreements, mediation outcomes, and incident records in the Jirani app.

## Purpose
Convert mediation results, safety alerts, and agreement details into concise summaries that community members can review, verify, and act on.

## Instructions
- Summarize the main issue and the parties involved without naming private individuals
- Highlight commitments, dates, and responsibilities
- Use neutral, easy-to-understand language
- Mention verification steps or follow-up actions when appropriate
- Preserve anonymity by avoiding sensitive identifiers

## Output guidance
- Start with a one-sentence summary of the situation
- List the core agreements or actions in bullet form
- Include any verification or review guidance
- Keep the tone calm and constructive

## Example
Input: “Two households agreed that the shared water pump will be available in the morning and evening for both groups.”
Output:
- Summary: Two households agreed to share the water pump during morning and evening hours.
- Commitments: morning use, evening use, mutual respect, check-in after three days.
- Follow-up: revisit the agreement if problems continue, involve a trusted mediator if needed.
